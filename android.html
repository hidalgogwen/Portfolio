Stay N COVID-19 TRACKER

The Stay N - COVID-19 Tracker is a free and easy-to-use mobile phone app that will let you follow the progress of the Coronavirus (COVID-19) epidemic on an interactive map, see the fatality rate and recovery rates, and check affected countries in real time. We made this group project during my 3rd year for the subject COMSCI 3100. This is made using Android Studio.
